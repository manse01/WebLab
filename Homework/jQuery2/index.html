Bora Bora- The South Pacific's Gem

Bora Bora is a Pacific island in the territory of French Polynesia, which is part of the Society Islands located exactly halfway between South America and Australia. Worldatlas.com calls it “arguably the most beautiful island on the planet” and “arguably the most famous island in the world.” Bora Bora is known for its breathtaking waters, exquisite resorts, and beautiful volcanic rock. The best times to go to Bora Bora is practically all twelve months of the year. With an average high in the mid 80's year round, Bora Bora is consistently the perfect place to visit.
