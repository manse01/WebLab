About

Seth Manilove is a recent graduate of the University of Miami.

The red sable is a specific type of dachshund with long, red-brown hair. The Dachshund was originally bred for the strength and tenacity to hunt badgers. In order to hunt these elusive, yet aggressive animals, the dachshund have to have a back that is long and narrow. The Germans envisioned the dogs to travel through long narrow tunnels to hunt badgers and other small rodents.
